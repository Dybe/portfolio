Development

Theme

For the Development project I made a simple react web app that lets users view and book selected travel experiences to various regions in Africa. I made the app  to remind myself of and expose my peers to the beauty and wonders of the continent I call home.

Usability & User Interface Design

Users see the experiences’  image previews, locations (by country and region)  and price. They can filter experiences by region and type of tourism or sort experiences by price.  If they want, they can book an experience by clicking a button.

I wanted to leverage the fact that my work is not graded on visual design to challenge myself to come up with a minimalistic interface that is still effective, efficient and engages the user.  This allowed me to appreciate the potential value of  informative text and uplifting /positive and lighthearted textual notifications/ feedback to the user experience.  For example, I use text to let the user know what experiences they are looking at according to their filters and to relay a lighthearted message if they have no bookings.  I tried my best to use text to make the user feel like the web app was talking to them.

See my work on GitHub and explore the web app. I hope you are fascinated by at least one of the experiences and add them to your wish list!

Conclusion and Learnings 

This project allowed me to solidify my understanding of fundamental react concepts such as components, state, props and data transfer between components. It also taught me that in addition to appealing visual design and typography, informative and positive textual content plays an important role in providing good user experience.
